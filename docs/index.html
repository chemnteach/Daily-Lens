Grey's Anatomy Star Eric Dane Dies at 53 From ALS
Actor Eric Dane, known for his role as Dr. Mark Sloan on ABC's Grey's Anatomy and appearances in HBO's Euphoria, has died at age 53 following a battle with ALS (amyotrophic lateral sclerosis), the most common form of motor neurone disease. The actor's death marks the loss of a prominent television figure from a progressive neurodegenerative condition.
